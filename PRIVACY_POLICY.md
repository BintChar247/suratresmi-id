## 5. Data Retention Policy

**Default Retention Period: 90 Days**

Personal data is retained for 90 days from the date of collection, after which it is securely deleted.

**Exceptions:**
- **Account data** (email, name): Retained until account deletion
- **Transaction records**: Retained for 7 years (for tax/accounting compliance)
- **Audit logs**: Retained for 1 year (for security and UU PDP compliance)
- **Support tickets**: Retained for 1 year (for dispute resolution)

**Upon Account Deletion:**
- All personal data is deleted within 30 days
- Transaction records retained for 7 years (anonymized)
- You may request earlier deletion if no legal obligation exists

## 7. User Rights Under UU No. 27/2022

You have the following rights:

### 7.1 Right to Access
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
- **Process:** Email dpo@suratresmi.id with "Access Request" in subject
- **Timeline:** We will respond within 30 days
- **Cost:** Free of charge

### 7.2 Right to Correction
You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Process:** Email dpo@suratresmi.id or update info in account settings
- **Timeline:** Correction made within 15 days
- **Cost:** Free

### 7.3 Right to Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten")
You can request deletion of your personal data.
- **Process:** Email dpo@suratresmi.id with "Deletion Request"
- **Exceptions:** 
  - Transaction records (7-year tax obligation)
  - Audit logs (1-year UU PDP requirement)
- **Timeline:** Deletion completed within 30 days of exceptions passing
- **Cost:** Free

### 7.4 Right to Data Portability
You can request your data in a portable format.
- **Format:** CSV or JSON export
- **Timeline:** 30 days
- **Cost:** Free

### 7.5 Right to Restrict Processing
You can request that we stop processing your data (while retaining it).
- **Process:** Email dpo@suratresmi.id
- **Effect:** Data retained but not processed (except legal obligations)
- **Timeline:** Restriction applied within 15 days

### 7.6 Right to Object
You can object to specific processing (e.g., marketing emails, usage analytics).
- **Process:** Unsubscribe link in email or account settings
- **Timeline:** Effective immediately for new requests

### 7.7 Right to Lodge a Complaint
If you believe your data rights are violated, you can file a complaint with the Indonesian Data Protection Authority (OJK).

## 8. Security Measures

We implement the following security practices:

### 8.1 Technical Security
- **Encryption:** All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Hashing:** Passwords hashed using bcrypt (never stored plain text)
- **Access Control:** Role-based access; admin access logged
- **Firewalls:** DDoS protection via Cloudflare
- **Intrusion Detection:** Monitoring for unauthorized access attempts

### 8.2 Organizational Security
- **Limited Access:** Only authorized staff access personal data
- **Data Processing Agreements:** All vendors sign strict DPAs
- **Incident Response:** Data breach notification within 72 hours (per UU PDP)
- **Security Audits:** Annual third-party security assessment

### 8.3 What We Can't Guarantee
- No system is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute protection
- Your responsibility: Use strong passwords, enable 2FA if available, don't share login credentials
